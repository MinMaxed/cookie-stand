Pat's Salmon Cookies!!!

Best cookies this Side of the Columbia

Pat's Salmon Cookies was founded in 1870. Back in the day, Pat made her cookies out of REAL Chinook Salmon,
as it was the only bountiful resource available to her. Today, our cookies are still made out of salmon,
in keeping with tradition, but also come in cod, bass, and trout flavors.
We've even started branching into King Crab Cakes, Clam Brownies, and Octo-pies.
